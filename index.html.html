About the Clinic

Caring for Kolkata's Smiles Since 2012

SmileCraft Dental is a modern, fully equipped dental clinic committed to making dental care comfortable, accessible, and effective for every patient.

Located in the heart of Kolkata, our clinic combines the latest dental technology with a warm, patient-first environment. From routine check-ups to complex procedures, every treatment is tailored to your unique needs.

Our Services

Complete Dental Care,
All Under One Roof

From preventive care to advanced cosmetic dentistry — we have every treatment you need.

🦷

Teeth Cleaning

Professional scaling & polishing to remove plaque and tartar, leaving your teeth sparkling clean and gums healthy.

🔬

Root Canal Treatment

Pain-free RCT using rotary endodontics — save your natural tooth and relieve severe toothache quickly.

🏅

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements using titanium implants — the gold standard in tooth restoration.

😁

Braces & Aligners

Metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear aligners (Invisalign) for perfectly aligned, confident smiles.

✨

Teeth Whitening

Professional bleaching for dramatically whiter teeth in a single visit. Safe, fast, and long-lasting results.

👶

Kids Dentistry

Gentle, child-friendly dental care in a calm environment — making first dental visits fun and fear-free.
